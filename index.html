vocaloid is a software program and kind of like a digital instrument! it works by plugging in the notes that you'd like the voice to sing, and assigning syllables to them. the end result is a computerized singing voice! vocaloid is one such program, and as the originator, is often used to refer to the whole vocal synthesizer community. however, there are many vocal synthesizer softwares to accomplish similar things, including synthesizer v and utau!
what is super cool and beautiful about vocal synths is that anyone can buy or download a voicebank and start making songs! though originally specialized for japanese, there are now voicebanks in many many languages! additionally, you can edit or "tune" the voicebank in a pretty much infinite combination of ways. this means that any two people, even using the same voicebank, can each create a totally unique sound. the result is an incredibly diverse community of creatives, making every type of music under the sun, all united by the love of the same characters!
